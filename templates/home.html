About this website

Hello! Welcome to our web app called Volunteerism. We made this website for the DubHacks hackathon at the University of Washington. We started at around 1:00 PM on Saturday (June 12, 2019) and finished at around 11:00 AM on Sunday (June 13, 2019). Our backend uses Python, Django, and PostgreSQL. Our frontend uses HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and some JavaScript and jQuery. We made Volunteerism to allow users to create, join, and organize different volunteer events around their communities. We hope you enjoy!
